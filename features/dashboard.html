Eco-Friendly Recommendations

Simple changes to reduce our energy footprint.

Power Management & Settings

  • Enable sleep mode on all computers to activate after 10 minutes of inactivity.
  • Configure machines to power off automatically overnight from 10 PM to 6 AM.
  • Disable screensavers, as modern monitors save more power by simply turning off.

Hardware Improvements

  • Prioritize laptops over desktops, as they consume significantly less energy.
  • When upgrading, replace old CRT or CCFL monitors with new LED/OLED models.
  • Use smart power strips to easily shut down peripherals (monitors, speakers) when a computer is turned off.

Behavioral Changes

  • Encourage students and staff to manually shut down computers if they will be away for more than an hour.
  • Launch an awareness campaign to highlight the environmental impact and promote green habits.
  • "Last one out turns off the lights" policy should extend to all lab equipment.
