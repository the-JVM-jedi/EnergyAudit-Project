Sustainable Computing: Energy Audit of Campus Labs

Measuring our energy consumption to build a greener future for campus IT.

Welcome to the Campus Energy Audit Portal

At the forefront of sustainable computing, our platform empowers students, faculty, and IT administrators to monitor and manage energy consumption across campus computer labs. Through real-time data tracking and insightful analytics, we uncover power usage patterns and highlight opportunities for eco-friendly improvements.

Built with responsible computing principles, this tool not only supports operational efficiency but also champions environmental stewardship. Together, let's drive smarter energy use and a greener future.
